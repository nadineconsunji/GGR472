Map Information

Right-hand corner

In the upper right hand corner, you can use the buttons to search for specific countries, zoom in and out of the map and make the map full screen.

Central, main map

Numerical measurements for each country are showcased using blue colour intensity. Darker blue countries have higher numberical values for what is being showcased. On the main map, hovering over a country allows you to see its individual, specific energy transition readiness scores including its composite index, transition readiness, and system performance scores. Double clicking on a country allows you to zoom to its level and returns a short summary of the country’s energy infrastructure and potential for an energy transition. To average the composite index, transition readiness and system performance of multiple countries, you can click on the countries of interest and press the ‘combine’ button in the left-hand sidebar.

Left-hand sidebar

Use the ‘filter map view’ items to toggle between transition measurements - composite index, transition readiness, and system performance. Use the ‘region’ items to focus on specific regions in Africa.
