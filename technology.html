Technology-Specific Preparedness

This map shows transition readiness across Africa by type of renewable energy technology. These are scores of how well a country is prepared to shift to clean energy across each of these technologies.

A score of 0 signifies low preparedness, while 100 represents full preparedness.

Map Instructions


  • Hover your mouse over a country to see its individual, specific clean energy capacity including its composite index, transition readiness and system performance scores
  • Double clicking on a country allows you to zoom to its geographic level and returns a short summary of the country's energy infrastructure and potential for an energy transition
  • To average the composite index, transition readiness or system performance of multiple countries to illustrate a specific region's capacity to achieve a clean energy transition, tick the 'new selection' box and click on countries of interest, then press the 'calculate average' button
  • Use the 'filter map view' items to toggle between transition measurements - composite index, transition readiness, and system performance
  • Use the ‘region’ items to focus on specific regions in Africa
  • Use the side bar button to slide the sidebar in and out of the screen
